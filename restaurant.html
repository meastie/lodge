Locally inspired,
riverside.

Seasonal plates from the valley's organic farms and ranches, a craft cocktail & wine bar, and views of the Applegate River — a remarkable table, outside the standard.

Our table gather & savor

Food & drink, thoughtfully sourced from the heart of Southern Oregon.

As a family of foodies, we built The Lindsay around the belief that the best experiences happen in beautiful places, over thoughtfully sourced food and drinks. Our kitchen partners with the organic farms, ranches and vineyards that surround us — the menu follows the season, and the river is always in view.

Join us for dinner, a craft cocktail at the bar, or a glass of Applegate Valley wine on the deck.
