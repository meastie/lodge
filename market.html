About the Market

Where the valley gathers.

Every Wednesday evening from May through September, the grounds of The Lindsay come alive with the Applegate Evening Market — an outdoor gathering celebrating the farms, makers, and musicians of the Applegate Valley.

Each week you'll find fresh produce from local farms, handcrafted goods, artisan products, and more — all while enjoying live music. The Lindsay Lodge bar is open throughout the evening, serving beer, wine and a bistro menu. You'll also find other food vendors from the area.

Bring your lawn chair, your neighbors, and your appetite.
