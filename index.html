Welcome to The Lindsay a river runs through it

A handcrafted log lodge on the banks of the Applegate River.

Set on a three-acre riverfront estate between Grants Pass and Medford near historic Jacksonville, The Lindsay is family-owned and built for forever memories. Every room opens onto a balcony with views of the Applegate River and the historic bridge.

Whether it's a reunion, wedding, retreat, or a romantic getaway in wine country — we specialize in creating memories.
